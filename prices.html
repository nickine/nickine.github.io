Grooming Price List

Prices are approximate and based on dogs with an even temperament and are regularly groomed (most breeds every 6-8 weeks). All prices are dependent on condition of coat and temperament of the dog and will be confirmed on day of appointment.

Small Dogs

example: Jack Russells, Yorkshire Terriers, Miniture Schnauzer, Shih Tzu

Bath & Tidy prices start from £20.00*
Full Groom prices start from £30.00*
* Prices based on short-haired breeds, condition and lenth of coat will affect cost

Medium Dogs

example: Cocker Spaniel, Staffordshire Bull Terrier, Beagle, West Highland Terrier

Bath & Tidy prices start from £25.00*
Full Groom prices start from £35.00*
* Prices based on short-haired breeds, condition and lenth of coat will affect cost

Large Dogs

example: German Shepherd, Japanese Akita, Alaskan Malamute

Bath & Tidy prices start from £45.00*
Full Groom prices start from £50.00*
* Prices based on short-haired breeds, condition and lenth of coat will affect cost
